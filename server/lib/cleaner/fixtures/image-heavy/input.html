Best Photos from Kyoto in Autumn

Kyoto in autumn is one of the most beautiful places on earth. The maple trees turn brilliant shades of red, orange, and gold, creating a stunning backdrop for the ancient temples and shrines.

The Golden Pavilion, or Kinkaku-ji, is perhaps the most iconic sight in Kyoto. During autumn, the golden structure is framed by brilliant red maples, creating a scene of extraordinary beauty.

Fushimi Inari is another must-visit destination. The thousands of vermillion torii gates create a striking contrast against the autumn foliage. Early morning visits offer the best light for photography.

The bamboo grove at Arashiyama is a unique experience. While the bamboo stays green year-round, the surrounding mountains display beautiful autumn colors that complement the grove.
