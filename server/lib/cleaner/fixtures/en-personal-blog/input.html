My Weekend Hike to Mount Takao

Last weekend I decided to take a break from coding and go hiking at Mount Takao, a beautiful mountain just west of Tokyo. The weather was perfect for a hike.

The trail starts at the base station and winds through a dense forest of cedar trees. The air is fresh and the sounds of the city quickly fade away as you ascend.

At the summit, you can see all the way to Mount Fuji on a clear day. I was lucky enough to get a perfect view. The round trip took about 3 hours at a comfortable pace.

I highly recommend Mount Takao for anyone looking for an easy day hike near Tokyo. It is accessible by train and the trails are well-maintained.
